Description

Health and Curls is a yearly event aimed at tackling natural hair, diet and general maternal and mens health issues. This years topics include maternal and fetal Health, losing baby weight, contraceptives, facts you need to know as a woman, the effects of diet on several illnesses, topics related to hair, etc. There will be lots of vendors, giveaways, good food, great music, etc Sign up for free and come spend a day with us. It will be worth your while. Early birds get served breakfast. To make any enquiries about vending, kindly email us at healthandcurlsgh@centrum.cz or whatsapp or call +420776693735 /+23324787751 See ya

FAQs

What are my transportation/parking options for getting to and from the event?

To get to the osu presby Hall, you just need to get into a car heading to Osu. All public buses pass right beside the Ebenezer Presby Church, which is where you will be getting off. There is a huge car park should you need to park your car in the premises of the church.

What can I bring into the event?

You dont need to bring any items to the event. Should you want to do some shopping, money will definitely be needed :)

How can I contact the organizer with any questions?

You can reach the organizers via email ( healthandcurlsgh@centrum.cz ) or call / whatsapp +420776693735 (Avery) , 0247877551 (Theo)

Do I have to bring my printed ticket to the event?

You dont need to print your ticket. Its enough to show up at the registration table.

Any giveaways?

Early birds get served hot chocolate and waffles.
Every attendee is entitled to enter the draw to win lots of amazing gifts.
